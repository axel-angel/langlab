split*

(split* s re)

Splits s on regexp re, but as opposed to string/split keeps the regexp matches in a resulting seq.

Similar effect can be achieved by using look-arounds, but its clumsy. http://stackoverflow.com/questions/19951850/split-string-with-regex-but-keep-delimeters-in-match-array
