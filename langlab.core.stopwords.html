langlab.core.stopwords

Module contains predefined sets of stopwords/articles for various languages and functions to operate on them.

All constants are lowercase.

(trans-drop-set drop-set tokens)

trans-drop-set-all-case

(trans-drop-set-all-case drop-set tokens)

Drop all elements of ‘tokens’ that are included in the ‘drop-set’. Ignore case. For ‘drop-set’ one of the module constants representing stopwords or articles can be used.
